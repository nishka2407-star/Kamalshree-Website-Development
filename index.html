Our Story

Where Heritage Meets
Modern Elegance

Kamal Shree was born from a passion for preserving the grandeur of Indian craftsmanship. Founded in the heart of Ahmedabad's textile district, we have spent over a decade curating and crafting ethnic wear that speaks to the soul of every celebration — from intimate mehendis to grand weddings.

Our collections blend time-honoured embroidery techniques — zardozi, resham, gota patti, and mirror work — with contemporary silhouettes that flatter the modern woman. Every piece in our store is selected with care, ensuring that when you wear Kamal Shree, you wear art.
